OG

Kanguva

Kanguva:- Chapter 1, also known and marketed as Kanguva: A Mighty Valiant Saga, is an upcoming Indian Tamil-language fantasy action film directed by Siva and produced by K. E. Gnanavel Raja, V. Vamsi Krishna Reddy and Pramod Uppalapati under the banners of Studio Green and UV Creations.

Game Changer

Game Changer is an upcoming Indian Telugu-language political action thriller film directed by S. Shankar in his Telugu directorial debut and written by Karthik Subbaraj.

Eagle

Eagle is an upcoming Indian Telugu-language action thriller film written and directed by cinematographer Karthik Gattamneni, who also previously directed Surya vs Surya. It is produced by T. G. Vishwa Prasad and Vivek Kuchibhotla, under People Media Factory.
